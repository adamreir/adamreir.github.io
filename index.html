I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Economics/FAIR, Norwegian School of Economics.

My research interests are in labor economics and economics of education. I am also interested in applied econometrics and inequality in the labor market.

I study heterogeneity in returns to fields of study and comparative advantage of multidimensional skills, mismatch in the college market, and job search with pay and non-pay job attributes.

In my work, I combine rich micro data with structural estimation and quasi-experimental methods, and apply natural language processing tools.
